Project

In this project, we were tasked with creating a new design for the mobile version of the parent cooperative Småtrollens' website. Our design approach was grounded in heuristic research.

Research

In this project, we employed heuristics as a methodology to assess the existing webpage. Our initial step involved a cursory examination of the website to gain an overview and determine which specific heuristics warranted further examination. After careful consideration, we selected three key heuristics for evaluation:

1.Consistency and Standards (Heuristic #4)

2.Error Prevention (Heuristic #5)

3.Aesthetic and Minimalistic Design (Heuristic #8)

These heuristics guided our evaluation process and provided a structured framework for identifying usability issues and opportunities for improvement in the design.

Subsequently, each team member independently assessed the website based on the three selected heuristics. To streamline the process of sharing and consolidating our individual findings, we generated "problem cards." These cards served as concise summaries of the identified issues, providing valuable information about the problems and aiding in the integration of our collective insights.

Desing process

The client provided us with creative freedom to conceive the new design, emphasizing several key attributes for the website:

- Easy to use

- Informative

- Nature feeling

- Calm

- Picture orienter

Armed with this information, we began to formulate a structural framework for the desired appearance and layout of the website.

We initiated the graphic design phase, centering our efforts on aligning with the client's preferences and adhering to the identified heuristics. Our primary objective was to create a design that was both clean and standardized, ensuring that users could navigate the website with the utmost ease and clarity.

Our client provided us with their current grapich profile including font style, logotyp and colour palette.

Our suggestion

We appreciated the colors and the ambiance they conveyed, but we made the decision to restrict our palette to shades of blue and green. We opted to lighten these colors to enhance contrast and achieve a more airy and uplifting atmosphere on the website.

Colours:

In addition, we made the choice to develop a new logo that embodies simplicity and modernity, with a timeless quality that ensures its relevance over time. The incorporation of mushrooms in the logo serves a dual purpose: it brings a touch of nature into the design while also introducing a playful and inviting element.

Logo:

For the font style, we made the decision to retain Arial for headers, but we introduced Gudea for the body text. This choice was made to maintain a light and airy feel throughout the website while ensuring readability and visual harmony between different text elements.

We added drop shadow on all pictures to get them to stand out more and create the picture oriented feeling our client aspierd.

To make it easy for the user to navigate on the website and to find what they were searching for we choose to have multiple menus.

Insights

Creating something from scratch can indeed be more challenging, but the reward is proportionally greater. I've also gained a deeper appreciation for the substantial effort required to develop something entirely new. There are numerous steps and a multitude of considerations necessary to achieve the comprehensive result one aspires to attain. Consequently, another valuable insight I've garnered is the paramount importance of the team. Having an additional set of eyes and perspectives in this type of project is truly invaluable.
