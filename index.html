About me

Who am I?

I am a twenty-something-year-old UX student from Gothenburg. I have almost finished my first year at IT-Högskolan, and now I'm looking forward to what's next to come.

So, why did I choose UX?

In the role of User Experience, you operate in the space between technology, people, and business. I find it an exciting challenge to one day be the person who drives the process of designing digital solutions with the user's needs and motivations in focus. Knowing that my designs can provide a positive experience for anyone who interacts with digital innovation motivates me to work hard to achieve the best possible results. I am an ambitious, analytical, and sociable person, qualities that I believe will be rewarding for future assignments.
